The History of Jamaican Curry

A beautiful fusion of East Indian heritage and Caribbean spirit.

Jamaican curry is uniquely distinct from Indian curry. Its roots trace back to the 17th century with the arrival of East Indian indentured workers to the island, who brought their traditional spice blending techniques with them.

Over generations, local Jamaican herbs like pimento (allspice), scotch bonnet peppers, and fresh thyme were combined with traditional turmeric, coriander, and cumin. This created the vibrant, bold, and yellow-hued seasoning celebrated worldwide today.
